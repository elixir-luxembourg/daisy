## Legal Basis (GDPR)
Legal basis is defined at the project level because GDPR ties the lawful basis to the purpose of a processing activity, not to individual datasets. DAISY Projects represent these processing activities. [rd-alliance.org]
A project must therefore specify:

Article 6 lawful basis (e.g., public interest/task in the public interest for universities, or legitimate interests for non‑public bodies). [research.m...ster.ac.uk]
Article 9 condition when processing special‑category data (e.g., scientific research under Art. 9(2)(j), or explicit consent). [research.m...ster.ac.uk]

If a project appears to require multiple different lawful bases, this indicates that it actually contains multiple processing activities, which must be split into separate DAISY Projects. GDPR requires one lawful basis per clearly defined purpose. [catalogue....-impact.eu]
