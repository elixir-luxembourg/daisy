DAISY

v{{ app_version }}

Data Information System for GDPR bookkeeping in biomedical research. Records what personal data is held, on what legal basis, and under whose stewardship.

First described in DAISY: A Data Information System for accountability under the General Data Protection Regulation, GigaScience, 2019.
